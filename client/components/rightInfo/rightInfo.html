INFORMATION

Please note that the closing date for Pre-Conference (Regular Price) registration is 9th of August 2016.

  1. From the Home Page click Start Registration 
  2. Select your Category
  3. Fill Registration Form following the 'On-Screen' instructions
  4. Select your preferred Payment Option (registration payment MUST be completed within 48 hours from filling the form or your application will be disregarded)
    • Pay Online – Payment will be processed immediately
    • Pay To the Bank – Payment will be processed subject to confirmation from the issuing bank 
  5. Upon successful payment a verification code via email/sms will be sent to you within 48 hours 
  6. A Personal Login Information will be issued you, from which you can preview activities regarding the Conference and also print your Payment Receipt and Participation Slip 
  7. Online registration using the Pay Online option Ends By 16th of August 2016 Pay To The bank option closes on 17th of August 2016 
  8. Prepare for your trip to Port Harcourt
