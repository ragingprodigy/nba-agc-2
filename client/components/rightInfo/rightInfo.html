INFORMATION

Please note that the rate for the pre-conference manual registration closes on the 21st of August 2015 while all pre-conference manual registration will end on the 7th of August 2015.

  1. From the ‘Home Page’ click ‘Register’ 
  2. Select your ‘Category’ 
  3. Enter your name as it appears on your ‘Call to Bar’ certificate and click ‘Apply’ 
  4. Select your correct detail from the ‘Search Result’ that appears 
  5. Fill out the ‘Registration Form’ that follows appropriately 
  6. Select your preferred ‘Payment Option’ (registration payment MUST be completed within 72 hours from filling the form or your application will be disregarded)
    • Pay Online – Payment will be processed immediately
    • Pay To the Bank – Payment will be processed subject to confirmation from the issuing bank 
  7. Upon successful payment a verification code via email/sms will be sent to you within 48 hours 
  8. A ‘Personal Login Information’ will be issued you, from which you can preview activities regarding the Conference and also print your ‘Payment Receipt’ and ‘Participation Slip’ 
  9. Online registration for ‘Pay On-hold’ option closes on 13 August 2015; Online registration for ‘Pay Online’ closes on 20 August 2015. 
  10. Prepare for your trip to ‘Abuja’
